# Jacobi-Matrix
## Definition
$$
J_f(x):=
\begin{pmatrix}
grad f_{1}(x) \\ \vdots \\ grad f_{m}(x)
\end{pmatrix}
=
\left(\begin{array}{}
\frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_1}(x) & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_n}(x) \\
\vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
\frac{\partial f_m}{\partial x_1}(x) & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_m}{\partial x_n}(x)
\end{array}\right)
\in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}
$$
